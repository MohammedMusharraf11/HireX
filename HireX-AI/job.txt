**Job Description: Machine Learning Engineer Intern**  

We are looking for a **Machine Learning Engineer Intern** to join our team and work on real-world AI applications. As an intern, you will collaborate with our data scientists and engineers to develop, test, and deploy machine learning models. You will gain hands-on experience in data preprocessing, model training, and optimization.  

### **Responsibilities:**  
- Assist in building and training machine learning models.  
- Preprocess and clean datasets for analysis.  
- Implement, test, and optimize ML algorithms.  
- Collaborate with engineers to deploy models into production.  
- Stay updated with the latest advancements in AI and ML.  


### **Requirements:**  
- Currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science, AI, or a related field.  
- Knowledge of Python and ML frameworks (TensorFlow, PyTorch, Scikit-learn).  
- Understanding of data structures, algorithms, and statistics.  
- Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP) is a plus.  
- Strong problem-solving and analytical skills.
